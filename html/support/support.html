Support This Initiative

This platform is a long-term knowledge project inspired by the Bhagavad Gita. Its purpose is not religious promotion, but to present the Gita as a source of practical wisdom, clarity, and inner understanding that can be explored by anyone.


This website is being developed in three phases:


  • Phase 1 (Current): Static, carefully structured, readable content.
  • Phase 2: Login system, feedback features, and discussion spaces.
  • Phase 3: An AI-based system inspired by the Bhagavad Gita.

Support for this project helps with:


  • Website maintenance and hosting
  • Design, performance, and structure improvements
  • Content expansion and future development
  • Sustaining long-term focus and effort

A portion of the support may also be used for personal sustainability, allowing continued learning and long-term commitment to this initiative.


Support is completely voluntary. There is no minimum amount and no restriction on access if you choose not to support.


Whether you choose to support or simply read and reflect, both are equally respected.
