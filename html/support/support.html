This platform is a long-term knowledge project inspired by the Bhagavad Gita. Its purpose is not religious promotion, but to present the Gita as a source of practical wisdom, clarity, and inner understanding accessible to everyone.

Project Vision & Roadmap

  • Phase 1 (Current): Static, carefully structured, readable content.
  • Phase 2: Login system, community feedback, and discussion spaces.
  • Phase 3: An AI-based system inspired by the Bhagavad Gita for deep interaction.

Why Your Support Matters
